Targeted Groups

The organization targets a number of groups in conducting its operations.

Inmates
Inmates

The agency assists the inmates with psychological support through counselling to break the silence in them and counselling helps this group of clientele to avoid sickness such as high blood pressure. Also the inmates who requests for bail are supported with the money, donations through prison visits and also evangelism for resilience and also as one of the aim of the agency and for the inmates to regret and be penitent before they go back into the community and to their families as well.

Ex Inmates
Ex Inmates

The agency helps the ex-inmates with family and community reintegration in unification with the counselling process by social workers. Also preparations of donations to the ex-inmates are made through resource mobilisation from churches or individuals. The agency provide halfway home for ex-inmates who have nowhere to go when they are released whilst social workers affiliated to the agency make contacts with the family of the client for reintegration process and also bus fare for this type of clients to their homes after conducting the process successfully and also grounding on the recorded information in the sessions conducted.

ZPCS Officers
ZPCS Officers

The agency works hand in hand with this clientele in the sense that this group provide information to the agency and the information provided helps the agency to facilitate the programs at prison and also the ZPCS officers offer information on what the prisoners need so that the agency provide the materials needed.

Community
Community

The agency works with the communities concertizing through awareness campaigns on health issues or any matter which needs the community to recognize or identify as a problem so that people in community will not end up as inmates. Also the agency works with the communities when conducting reintegration.

Victims of Crime
Victims of Crime

The agency works with the victims of crime through facilitating the programs generated by the agency to help the clients such as reconciliation programs. Also counselling is provided to solve the problems identified.

Inmates' Dependants
Inmates Dependants

The agency focus mainly on children of inmates by providing necessary items the children need and also social workers facilitate the programs such as CPP which covers all areas that the child might need for assistance. The parent/guardian are included by assisting them with food support, livelihood support and also counselling in combination with the roles of social worker to this type of group served by the agency.

Formed in 2019 POST encompasses all aspects of prison work from crime awareness, rehabilitation , reintergration and human rights reforms. POST was formed to bridge the gap between for fellowship and Christian evangelism in prisons of Zimbabwe as well as embracing the aspect of human reformation , transformation and development.
